How to run this game

  1. Download the game file above and print enough cards for your players (landscape, letter paper — colored paper works great)
  2. Open the Spotify or Apple Music playlist and hit Shuffle Play — play about 60 seconds of each song
  3. Hand out cards and (optionally) set up prizes for the winners
  4. Players mark off songs as they hear them — call a Line, 4 Corners, or full Blackout, or make up your own Bingos!
  5. Use the included callsheet to verify winners
